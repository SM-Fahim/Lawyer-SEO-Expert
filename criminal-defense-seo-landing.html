Technical SEO for Crisis Situations

When someone's searching for a criminal defense attorney on their phone from a parking lot at 2 AM, every second counts. Technical issues that might be minor for other businesses can be catastrophic for criminal defense firms.

Mobile-First Is Everything

78% of criminal defense searches happen on mobile devices. Your site must be perfect on mobile:

  • One-tap calling: Phone number prominently displayed and clickable
  • Sub-2-second load time: Use tools like GTmetrix to test
  • Large, tappable buttons: CTA buttons should be at least 44px
  • Readable fonts: Minimum 16px font size for body text
Schema Markup for Lawyers

Help Google understand your content with structured data:

  • Attorney schema: Mark up lawyer profiles and practice areas
  • Review schema: Display star ratings in search results
  • Local business schema: Include address, phone, hours
  • FAQ schema: Get featured snippet opportunities

Critical Error to Avoid:

Never use contact forms as the primary way to get in touch. People in crisis want to talk to a human immediately, not fill out a form and wait for a response.
