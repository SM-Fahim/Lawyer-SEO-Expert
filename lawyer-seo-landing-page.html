Website Auditing

Our comprehensive website audit dives deep into your law firm's online presence, examining technical SEO elements like site speed, mobile-friendliness, and security. We identify broken links, crawl errors, and other issues that can negatively impact your search engine rankings and user experience. We also analyze your content, keyword usage, and site architecture to ensure optimal performance and visibility.

Keyword Research & Competitor Analysis

We utilize advanced SEO tools and industry best practices to uncover the most relevant and valuable keywords potential clients use when searching for legal services in your area. We analyze your competitors' online strategies, identifying their strengths and weaknesses to develop a data-driven SEO strategy that gives your firm a competitive edge.

On-Page SEO

We optimize every aspect of your website's on-page elements, including title tags, meta descriptions, header tags, image alt text, and internal linking structure. We ensure your content is strategically optimized for relevant keywords, improving search engine visibility and user engagement.

Content Writing

Our team of experienced legal content writers crafts compelling, informative, and SEO-friendly content that showcases your legal expertise and builds trust with potential clients. We create blog posts, articles, practice area pages, and other content that resonates with your target audience and drives organic traffic to your website.

Link Building

We employ ethical and effective link-building strategies to earn high-quality backlinks from reputable legal directories, industry publications, and other authoritative sources. We focus on building a natural and diverse link profile that improves your website's domain authority and search engine rankings.

Google My Business Management

We optimize your Google My Business (GMB) profile to ensure your law firm appears prominently in local search results, Google Maps, and the "Local Pack." We manage your GMB listing, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information, engaging with customer reviews, and leveraging GMB features to attract more local clients.
